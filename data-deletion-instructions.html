User Data Deletion

Instructions for Alperen Market

We are committed to protecting your privacy. You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data associated with our application. Please follow one of the methods below to remove your data.

Method 1: Remove via Facebook

You can remove our application from your Facebook account, which will automatically trigger the deletion of your data from our systems.

  1. Navigate to your Facebook profile and go to "Settings & Privacy", then click on "Settings".
  2. In the left sidebar, find and click on "Apps and Websites".
  3. Locate Alperen Market in the list of active apps.
  4. Click the "Remove" button next to the app. A confirmation pop-up will appear. Confirm that you wish to remove the app.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you prefer, you can send us a data deletion request directly. Please send an email to our support team, and we will process your request within 72 hours.
