Privacy Policy for Our Facebook App

This Privacy Policy describes how our Facebook application collects, uses, and shares your information.

Information We Collect

When you use our Facebook application, we may collect the following information provided by Facebook:

  • Your basic public profile information (such as your name, profile picture, and user ID).
  • Your email address, if you have given us permission to access it.
  • Any other information you grant permission for when you install the application.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • To communicate with you, such as sending you updates or responding to your inquiries.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data from our app, you can remove the app from your Facebook profile. You can also contact us at ibrahimsekerhalil@gmail.com to request that we delete your data.

To remove the app, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook profile's "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings".
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites" in the left sidebar.
  3. Find our app in the list and click "Remove".

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
